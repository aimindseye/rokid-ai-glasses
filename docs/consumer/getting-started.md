# Getting Started

## Before pairing

- Install the current Hi Rokid app.
- Charge the glasses.
- Enable Bluetooth and Wi-Fi.
- Review requested permissions.
- Create or sign in to a Rokid account when prompted.

## Pair through Hi Rokid

Use the in-app device flow rather than treating the glasses only as a generic
Bluetooth headset. Hi Rokid manages binding, firmware, AI, media, and device
settings.

A device may remain bound to the original account until it is unbound by the
owner.

## Verify after pairing

1. The glasses appear connected in Devices.
2. Audio output and microphone pickup are stable.
3. Camera/media permissions match your intended use.
4. The preferred AI base model is selected.
5. The firmware version is visible.
6. The local-model page accepts or rejects the phone clearly.

## Firmware

The firmware page is connection-gated:

1. connect the glasses;
2. open Devices;
3. open the glasses system/firmware view;
4. check the displayed version;
5. press **Check for Updates** if needed;
6. do not interrupt an installation.

## Privacy

AI, camera, translation, maps, and media features can involve sensitive
content. Disable permissions and services you do not need.
