# Evidence Levels

## Official

Published by Rokid in a product page, support article, SDK document, app-store
listing, or vendor-controlled repository.

## Observed

Directly reproduced on controlled hardware or derived from captured evidence.

## Inferred

The best explanation supported by observations, but not directly visible.

## Unverified

Included for discovery but not tested on the non-display Style model.

## Interpretation rules

- “Not observed” does not mean “does not exist.”
- An opaque route identifier is not proof of a specific public model version.
- A loaded local model is not proof that the final answer was generated locally.
- Operator wording errors are not ASR failures.
- Small latency samples are not statistical benchmarks.
- Evidence gaps must remain explicit.
